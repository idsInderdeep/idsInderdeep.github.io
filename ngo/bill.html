About Us

The main objective of the organization is the protection of natural resources such as water, vegetation and soil as well as the promotion of dynamic education. Swarnayug Foundation is working for villagers for weaker and backward sections to improve their life standard, to educate and to strengthen them. As Swarnayug Foundation has adopted the participatory development process in the area and is performing the key role in rehabilitation and development process in the area involving itself in various progammes and activities.
